Reminder app for your daily medication. Easy to use and respects your privacy.

------------

Ideal if you need to take your medicine every morning. Daily Pill shows a
reminder on your lock screen which is visible when you pick up the phone after
you wake up. Respond with the press of a single button, without unlocking.

If you need need to take your medicine on an empty stomach, this app also
remembers the exact time when you last took it.

Daily Pill is free and open source software.
It does not display ads, and does not collect your personal information.
